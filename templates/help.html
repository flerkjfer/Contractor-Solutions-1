Help

Thank you for using Contractor Solutions! This is the help page. You may begin by logging in or by registering! To register, click on register and enter your user name and password (don't forget to store important passwords somewhere safe). Select your role as a client or contractor, it depends on what you're looking for! Looking for jobs? Register as Contractor. Looking for a solution? Register as a Customer.

For Clients

  • Create a Job Request: Go to “Create New Job Request” from your dashboard. Fill in the service type, description, and details.
  • View Your Existing Requests: Click “View My Job Requests” to see all open, in-progress, or completed jobs.
  • Approve Contractor Requests: When a contractor wants to take your job, it will appear under “Pending Contractor Requests.” You can approve or reject them from your dashboard.
  • Rate & Pay Contractors: After approving completion of a job acquired by a contractor, you can submit a rating and payment amount for the contractor.

For Contractors

  • View Available Jobs: Your dashboard lists all open jobs you can apply for.
  • Request to Take a Job: Click “Request Job” on any available job. The client must approve before you can begin.
  • View Your Earnings & Rating: Your dashboard shows your total earnings and average rating.

Common Questions

  • I don’t see any available jobs. This usually means all jobs are taken or completed. Check back later.
  • I can't see job requests on my dashboard. Ensure you're logged in with the correct account type (Client vs Contractor).
